Character Artist

Senior Character Artist Independent London based PC/Console developer is looking for a talented Senior Character Artist to play a key role in the creation of photo-realistic characters for their upcoming AAA project.

Apply
Desired Skills and Experience:
  • Experience working on a published AAA title.
  • Superb understanding of high-end character modelling, sculpting and PBR materials.
  • Unreal Engine experience
  • Marvelous Designer
  • Substance Painter / Designer is an advantage.
  • n-depth understanding of human and animal anatomy
Key Responsibilities:
  • Working closely with the art team to improve workflows and processes.
  • Highly accurate modelling with superb attention to detail.
  • Unreal Engine experience
  • Working closely with the art team to improve workflows and processes.
  • Collaborating with other disciplines such as lighting and VFX artists, graphics programmers and level designers to create a AAA game experience.
  • n-depth understanding of human and animal anatomy
What's Offered:
  • Using production design art packs and concept art to create highly detailed character models.
  • Highly accurate modelling with superb attention to detail.
  • Good understanding of real-time hair creation and knowledge of skin shaders.
  • Working closely with the art team to improve workflows and processes.
  • Taking ownership of larger art tasks and seeing them through to completion.
